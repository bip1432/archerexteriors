At Archer Exteriors, we understand that choosing the right decking material for your new deck can be overwhelming. Here are a few factors to consider when making your decision:
  • Budget: The cost of decking can vary greatly depending on the materials chosen. Wood is typically less expensive than composite or PVC decking.
  • Maintenance: Some decking materials require more maintenance than others. Wood decking needs to be stained or sealed regularly to prevent rot and decay. Composite and PVC decking require less maintenance but may need to be cleaned periodically.
  • Durability: Different decking materials have different levels of durability. Composite and PVC decking are more resistant to moisture, insects, and rot than wood decking.
  • Aesthetics: The look and feel of your deck is important. Different decking materials come in a variety of colors and textures, so be sure to choose a material that complements your home's exterior.
At Archer Exteriors, our team of experts can help you choose the right decking material for your needs and budget. Contact us today to learn more about our decking services.

The timeline for installing a new deck can vary depending on several factors such as the size of the deck, the materials used, and the complexity of the design. However, at Archer Exteriors, we strive to complete all our projects as efficiently as possible without compromising quality.

Typically, the installation process for a standard deck can take anywhere from two to four weeks. This includes the time it takes to obtain necessary permits, preparing the site for construction, building the deck frame, installing the decking, and finishing touches.

We understand that having construction work done at your home can be disruptive, which is why we work closely with our clients to schedule the project at a time that is convenient for them. Our team of skilled professionals will also keep you updated on the progress of the project to ensure that you are aware of any changes or delays.

If you have any specific questions about the timeline for your deck installation or any other renovation projects, please don't hesitate to contact us. We'd be happy to discuss your options and provide you with a detailed estimate of the project timeline and cost.

The optimal time to install a new deck is during the late spring, summer, or early fall when the weather is warm and dry. This allows for proper curing of the wood and ensures that the sealant or stain properly adheres to the surface. However, our team at Archer Exteriors can install decks year-round as long as the weather permits. We will work with you to find a time that fits your schedule and the weather conditions.
