Our Story

Our Journey & Story

For 40 years, Archer Exteriors continues to establish itself as a pioneer in the remodeling industry. Our company was founded on the premise of quality work at a fair cost. We are committed to exceeding our customer's expectations by providing the highest quality, service, and communication during every aspect of a project.

Tom Archer, the company's owner and founder, started a small roofing and siding business in South Jersey. His dedication to 100% client satisfaction and superior workmanship sets Archer Exteriors apart from the competition. Tom's wealth of experience working with contractors, homeowners, suppliers, and estimators gives him the ability to grow his company and expand Archer's Exteriors nationwide: from the mountains of Colorado to the Florida Panhandle. His thorough understanding of the rules and regulations of the construction industry have helped him become a leader in home renovation while continuing to provide exceptional service to the community.
